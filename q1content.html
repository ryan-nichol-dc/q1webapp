Question One - THE PAYROLL CAT!


Disaster has struck the C.A.T. Corporation Payroll Division! The lead payroll practitioner, Ms. Whiskers, accidentally misplaced her beloved cat, Catnip, amidst a sea of GUIDs! (I did not know this was possible either). The payroll system, being quirky and overly sentimental, refuses to process payments until Catnip is found.

In order to find the beloved Catnip, you must scour through the GUID sea.

  1. Strip each GUID of all its characters apart from c, a or t.
  2. The first GUID found that spells 'cat' is where your will find Catnip!
  3. Sum all the digits of the GUID as your final answer, and Catnip will be set free!!

Example Input:

c9065d99-ac24-46bc-b5dc-10dbd4ad9e80
3d87c0f0-d5e5-401b-9da2-67e692fc771f
42b3db27-61ca-473y-b12g-fb3g71bt2b01
4b5857fc-778f-4976-89bc-ffcfe542b681

1. Strip all the characters apart from c, a or t

    GUID 0: caccca
    GUID 1: cacasdf
    GUID 2: cat
    GUID 3: ccc

2. GUID 2 has the 'cat'!
3. Sum the digits of GUID 2 4+2+3+2+7+6+1+4+7+3+1+2+3+7+1+2+1
4. Final answer is 56
